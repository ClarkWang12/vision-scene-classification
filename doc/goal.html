Goal

The aim of this assignment is to perform image classification into different natural scene categories by using two methods: baseline method with spatial pyramid features and SVM classification(histogram intersection kernel) and the LLC method with locality-constrained linear coding, max-pooling and SVM classification(linear kernel).
